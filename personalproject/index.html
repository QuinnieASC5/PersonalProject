Lifestyle In Brookline

    What it's like
    Being in Brookline is hard work. They don't just prepare you physically but mentally too. There are many jobs you can do there such as working at an Ice Cream shop and helping kids at the Rec. At Brookline there are many people and kids to meet. Kids run around this area like crazy and we host event over the summer too. At the park we do movie nights every Thursday. That's where you can really meet people if the same intrest as you.
    Sports
    Football is our most celebrated sport and we pride ourself on it. With nine Superbowl wins and playoff appearances every year. The Brookline Knights are one of the best teams in their league. We don't just have a great football team but we also have an awesome baseball team that's been around for 67 years. We also have basketball, soccer, and deck hockey.
    History
    Brookline History really starts at our "The Cannon". It was given to us to show our cares for the veterans. The cannon sits on the small island situated between Brookline Boulevard, Queensboro and Chelton Avenues. But this cannon left it's spot July 8, 2017 and came back October. It was gone becuase a PAT bus had ran through the same park and taken the cannon with it. They restored it and given it a new code of paint. The original cannon was a 4.7 inch Gun M1906 but later it became to old to be restored so we replaced it with the model 1917 Schneider 155mm Howitzer.
